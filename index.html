Welcome!

I am a Quantitative Researcher at Citadel Securities.

I got my PhD degree in statistics in 2021 from the Department of Statistics at Harvard University, where I was advised by Professor Lucas Janson and Professor Jun Liu. My research interests include high-dimensional statistics, nonparametric statistics and Markov chain Monte Carlo. Prior to Harvard, I obtained a B.S. in statistics from the Special Class for the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China (Chinese: 中科大少年班) in 2016. I spent one summer at Citadel Securities as a quantitative researcher in 2020.
