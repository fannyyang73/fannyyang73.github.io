Fei Yang (杨菲)

Fei is currently a Master's student at Beihang University, majoring in statistics. She was advised by Professor Shanshan Wang. Previous to that, she obtained her Bachelor’s degree in Engineering from Beihang University with the First Class Honors in 2020. In 2019, Fei joined a machine learning summer school in the Department of Electrical & Computer Science at the Israel Institute of Technology with the CSC scholarship.

Fei is passionate about variable selection, high-dimensional statistics, and machine learning related topics. Her Master's dissertation proposed a new type of variable selection framework for high-dimensional generalized additive model and verified its effectiveness using the cardiomyopathy microarray dataset. Her second research worked on robust optimal reconciliation for hierarchical time series forecasting with M- estimation, which has been under major revision after submission to the International Journal of Forecasting.

Her goal is to tackle issues with high-dimensionality and solve real-world problems. She joined the Epidemiology Lab at National Center for Cardiovascular Diseases in Fuwai Hospital in 2022. Fei also gained data analysis experience in the industry , e.g. Tencent, Xiaomi, and BMW China.
